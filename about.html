About Shrinath Earthing Solutions India Pvt. Ltd.
Shrinath Earthing Solutions India Pvt. Ltd is a leading Indian technical consultancy specialising in Power System Earthing measurements, design, ROEP assessment, policy, training and R&D. Previously trading as Strategy & Solutions Ltd and Earthing Measurements Ltd it has provided support to Indian DNOs and the wider electricity distribution, transmission and generation industries from past Eight Years.

Nearly all the company’s power protectors have the SABS mark of approval, and many of these products are specified by major users, such as mobile telephone networks, mining houses and the petroleum industry, to name a few. During the infancy stage of the mobile telephone industry, various telephone networks made use of our firm's expertise and protection devices to protect their installations from lightning and surge damage. Today, their products and expertise are still being used.

An unparalleled level of progressive engineering support and experience is built into each lightning and surge protection product and grounding solution. Shrinath Earthing Solutions products are manufactured to ISO® 9001:2008 and are subjected to rigorous field and laboratory testing. Today, as one of the most respected earthing consultancies in the India , we continue to develop, grow, challenge ideas, innovate and most importantly, service the needs of our increasing and valued client base.
